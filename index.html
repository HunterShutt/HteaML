Why I Like To Code

This post was made on 12-12-2014

Well, the reason I love coding is kind of silly actually. I play games, just like any other teen. I play Garry's Mod, CS:S, DayZ SA and mod, etc... Here's the story, I was played Garry's Mod one day and saw one of the players in a server I was on. He had a huge cube on his head and when he clicked mouse one (left mouse), the cube would smash on the ground, killing anything in its path. I was amazed at how he could do it. Another player had a shiny small circular thing flying around him glistfully in the air. I asked them how, they responded with "E2". E2 stands for Expression 2.

Expression 2 is like a notepad that you use special code to create things. I've learned a lot from Expression 2 and found that I had so much fun coding what ever I was coding. Even if it was horrible. Ever since I fell in love with anything to do with random bits of letters that makes something. I then used Google to find other languages that I could take advantage of. One of them was HTML/CSS. I learned them all. I know some JavaScript, and all HTML and CSS basics. I have an amazing time making them and if that could be my job. Then I would be having the time of my life and getting paid, amazing.
